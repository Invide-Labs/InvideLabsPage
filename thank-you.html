FAQ

Why should I consider Invide to hire engineers from the starting phase of my business?
One mediocre engineer as first hire can kill the startup. For bigger companies it may not matter much but for startups it makes the difference between success or shut down. We help you hire the perfect first engineers and work with them successfully. Period.
How much qualified Inviders are?
Our screening process is tougher than harvard. We have laser focus on one type of engineers, who fit as first engineers for startups. We perform thorough practical tests in a simulated environment. We judge technical skills, collaboration & communication skills, attitude, leadership qualities. You might picture them as "getting things done" people who have 3-5 yrs experience.
Do I have any say in selection of Invider for my team?
Yes. First, we hand-pick the right person for you based on your requirement. Then we arrange an intro call for you with the selected team member. Only after your approval the team member is assigned.
Can I communicate with the Inviders directly?
Absolutely. It is your team, you decide the rules and we are there to support you not block you in any way.
Can I hire them on my payroll?
Absolutely. But only after paying us a small Finder's fee. You understand that we do need to get paid to run our continuos operations of hunting rising stars, training them to become good to great team members, support their growth and most importantly to make sure that you onboard them smoothly, in a positive and motivated way.
How much time does it take to hire an Invider?
Generally it takes a week. It includes time spent in understanding requirement, hand-picking talent, confirming availability and scheduling call. If it seems to take more than that for specific profile, we let you know proactively.
I have more questions, what is the fastest way to reach Invide support?
Through WhatsApp
Do I have to work with Inviders before hiring on our payroll?
Yes. This is highly recommended and mandatory for now to make sure that you succeed. You can rely upon our skill testing done through practical tests/projects but we cannot guarantee culture fit, even when you and the talent are the best among crowd, success as a team can be tested only when you work together. This is the only sureshot way to avoid mistakes(startups can't afford bad hire, even when it is about culture misfit). And we have a great way to do so through subscription hiring model and then providing a smooth transition to full time on your payroll.

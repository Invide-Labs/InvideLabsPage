FAQ


Am I eligible?

  • To be eligible you must have contributed significantly to at least one tech product(personal or professional). All Inviders are smart, have strong fundamentals, problem solving skills and experience of building software/apps. Each Invider is at least 3x productive than an average developer. Reading this article about rejection at Invide Labs will help get right perspective about it.

How much time does screening takes?

  • Generally it takes 2-7 weeks to start the screening process. From there it takes around 3 weeks to complete the process till we let you know our final result. It takes time, hence do read whether you're eligible for application.

How do I increase my chances of selection?

  • Get reference from existing Inviders, contribute to our instant help group and open source projects. Don't tell, show!

I'm a fresher, can I join?

  • No. It is going to take time to be prepared to be an Invider. Here's what you can do. Choose an area of interest, study about it in depth, practice, build stuff, solve real business problems. When you feel confident enough to teach someone else about the same, that is the right time to apply to us. You'll find tons of course material out there to study. We also have recently complied a course to learn front-end web development. All the best!

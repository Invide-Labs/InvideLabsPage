How much time does screening takes?

  • Generally it takes 2-7 weeks to start the screening process. From there it takes around 3 weeks to complete the process till we let you know our final result. It takes time, hence do read whether you're eligible for application.

Am I eligible for application?

  • All Inviders are smart, have strong fundamentals, problem solving skills and experience of building software/apps. Each Invider is at least 3x productive than an average developer.

  • Get reference from existing Inviders, contribute to our open source projects. Don't tell, show!
